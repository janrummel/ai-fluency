## Shared Foundation: L1–L4

Everyone starts here. Four levels, five lessons each, each with its own paradigm shift.

### L1 — First Steps with AI
**Paradigm shift:** "AI is not Google"

| Lesson | Topic |
|---|---|
| 1 | What Is Generative AI? |
| 2 | Tools Overview: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot |
| 3 | First Conversations |
| 4 | Understanding Limits: Hallucinations, Knowledge Cutoff, Privacy |
| 5 | Do's and Don'ts |

### L2 — Intentional Prompting
**Paradigm shift:** "From questions to instructions"

| Lesson | Topic |
|---|---|
| 1 | The Anatomy of a Good Prompt |
| 2 | Prompting Techniques: Zero-Shot, Few-Shot, Chain-of-Thought |
| 3 | Iteration as Method |
| 4 | Task Types: Writing, Analyzing, Summarizing |
| 5 | Good vs. Bad Prompts |

### L3 — Context as Infrastructure
**Paradigm shift:** "Invest once, benefit continuously"

| Lesson | Topic |
|---|---|
| 1 | Persistent Context — Explain Your Job Once |
| 2 | Claude Projects: Your First Persistent Workspace |
| 3 | Custom GPTs: Building Specialized AI Assistants |
| 4 | M365 Copilot: AI Embedded in Your Workflow |
| 5 | System Prompt Design: The Art of Good Instructions |

### L4 — AI as Coworker
**Paradigm shift:** "From answers to autonomous work"

| Lesson | Topic |
|---|---|
| 1 | From Chat to Delegation: The Autonomy Spectrum |
| 2 | Claude Cowork: The Desktop Agent |
| 3 | ChatGPT Agent Mode: AI on the Web |
| 4 | Calibrating Trust |
| 5 | Compliance Basics: What You Need to Know |

## Paths After L4

After the shared foundation, choose the path that fits your role. All three paths are equally valid — none is "higher" than another.

### Maker — For Builders
*Technically curious, developers, solo founders*

**Module 1: AI-Assisted Development**
IDE integration (Cursor, Windsurf, GitHub Copilot), Claude Code, pair programming with AI, Git & CLI basics.

**Module 2: Building Without Code**
Lovable, Bolt, v0, Replit Agent — from idea to working app in natural language. When no-code, when code, when both.

**Module 3: Designing Your Own AI Systems**
CLAUDE.md as a "constitution," skills, memory architecture, MCP, quality gates, context engineering, agentic AI in enterprise.

### Coworker — For Integrators
*Knowledge workers maximizing AI in their daily work*

**Module 1: Cowork Mastery**
Advanced connectors (Slack, Notion, Drive, CRM), multi-tool workflows, recurring tasks, troubleshooting.

**Module 2: Productivity Systems**
Prompt libraries, AI in meetings, documentation, communication (email, reports, presentations).

**Module 3: Team Enablement**
Peer coaching, sharing best practices, team-wide standards, measuring and communicating AI impact.

### Leader — For Decision-Makers
*Executives, strategists, decision-makers*

**Module 1: AI Strategy**
Build vs. buy, vendor selection, ROI assessment, AI readiness.

**Module 2: Governance & Compliance**
EU AI Act (detail), GDPR, agentic AI compliance, risk management frameworks.

**Module 3: Culture & Enablement**
Driving adoption, understanding resistance, clarifying responsibility, building AI fluency across the organization.
