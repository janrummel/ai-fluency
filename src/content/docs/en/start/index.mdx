## What is AI Fluency?

AI Fluency is an open learning journey for knowledge workers. From your first conversation with ChatGPT to systematic collaboration with AI — at your own pace, with practical exercises.

## Who is it for?

For anyone who wants to work with AI professionally — whether you're in marketing, sales, finance, legal, or engineering. No technical background required.

## The Learning Journey

| Level | Topic | Paradigm Shift |
|-------|-------|----------------|
| **L1** | [First Steps](../l1-first-steps/01-what-is-ai/) | AI is not Google |
| **L2** | [Intentional Prompting](../l2-intentional-prompting/01-prompt-anatomy/) | From questions to instructions |
| **L3** | [Context as Infrastructure](../l3-context-infrastructure/01-persistent-context/) | Invest once, benefit continuously |
| **L4** | [AI as Coworker](../l4-ai-coworker/01-from-chat-to-delegation/) | From the text box to the task queue |

Each level builds on the previous one. You can check the [Level Check](../start/level-check/) anytime to find your starting point.

## Open and free

AI Fluency is an open-source project. All content is freely available and regularly updated.
